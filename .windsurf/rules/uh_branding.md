# University of Houston Branding Guidelines

All UI and design elements must adhere to the official University of Houston (UH) branding guidelines.

## 1. Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **UH Red:** `#C8102E` (Mandatory for primary actions, headers, and branding)
- **White:** `#FFFFFF`

### Secondary Colors
- **Black:** `#000000`
- **Slate:** `#54585A`

### Accent Colors (Use sparingly, < 30%)
- **Brick:** `#91272D`
- **Gold:** `#8B6F4B`
- **Ochre:** `#C5852B`

---

## 2. Typography

- **Headlines:** Use **League Gothic** (Sans-serif, bold, condensed) for high-impact headlines.
- **Body Copy:** Use **Source Sans 3** for general text and versatility.
- **Formal/Serif:** Use **Crimson** for academic or formal sections.
- **Fallback:** If brand fonts are unavailable, use system sans-serif (`Inter`, `system-ui`).

---

## 3. Design Aesthetic

- **Tone:** Professional, bold, and aspirational.
- **Elements:** Use geometric shapes, clean lines, and clear hierarchy.
- **Spacing:** Maintain generous "clear space" around logos and maintain a clean grid layout.
- **Icons:** Use simple, flat, single-color icons (e.g., FontAwesome or similar).

---

## 4. Logo Usage

- **Clear Space:** Always maintain clear space around the UH logo equal to the height of the 'H' in the mark.
- **Integrity:** Never stretch, distort, or change the colors of the UH signature marks.
- **Placement:** The UH Signature Mark should typically be placed in the header (left-aligned) or footer.
